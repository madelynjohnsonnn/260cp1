OUR INSIDE JOKES ABOUT FLOWERS

Branson is the funniest dude. We have a joke about flowers. We send each other pictures of ourselves with flowers in front of our faces. It's something that I don't want to forget. It started right after he got home from his mission, but I bet it would have happened on his mission if he had been mean enough to cover his face up with a flower;) So at our engagement shoot, Mom had us put the wildflowers in our faces. Too funny!
